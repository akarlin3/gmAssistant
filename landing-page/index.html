Symphony of the Three Books

We didn't invent these workflows; we just unified them. Gamemaster Assistant binds three award-winning methodologies together so they reinforce one another perfectly during play.

01

Lazy Dungeon Master

Build your prep around Mike Shea's legendary 8-step framework. Focus strictly on secrets, monster stats, fantastic locations, and key NPCs. Stay out of over-planning traps.

02

Collaborative Campaign

Establish safety guidelines (lines/veils), compile shared world facts, structure conflicts, and track faction clock progressions generated together during Session Zero.

03

Proactive Roleplaying

Focus on PC-driven goals. Map character ambitions directly into the prep panel, allowing you to tie upcoming secrets and locations back to player motivation.

The Tableside Engine

Beyond methodology, Gamemaster Assistant packs the ultimate tableside utility belt. Designed to run your sessions in real-time, keep your data isolated, and completely prevent planning fatigue.

Tableside Helpers

Roll instantly on custom tables, trigger inline NPC trait inspires, and browse structured NPC logs (including stats, motives, and visual details) in a single click.

Encounter Budgets

Calculate XP thresholds and combat difficulty ratings (Trivial, Easy, Medium, Hard, Deadly) live. Balanced dynamically for your party's level and size.

1.5s Auto-Save

Edit notes on your laptop and read them live on your phone. Auto-saves to Google Cloud Firestore exactly 1.5 seconds after you stop typing for seamless multi-device sync.

Complete Portability

Your campaign belongs to you. Export and import entire campaign templates as raw JSON files for complete offline ownership, backups, and zero platform lock-in.
